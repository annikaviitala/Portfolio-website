Developing Vare workshop usability

Service Design | School Project

Intensive course school project, goal was to get familiar with design fundamentals and the most common service design frameworks and methodology.
In the project we wanted to make the Väre workshop service path smoother in the view point of reservations and opening times. I ended up doing most of the work by myself even though this was intended to be a group project, due to my groupmates not responding to messages or participating in meetings.

My tasks included:
Designing and conducting a survey for ARTS students about preferred opening times and other practicalities to identify problems and user path in Vare workshop reservation service process.
Designing a solution for better reservation and availability functions based on survey answers.
Presenting the solution for the class in the end of the course.

Evaluation:
- Grade: 3/5
- Good work
- Improvement points: argumentation based on user research, a narrow view of the service path and the service structure as a whole. It is represented through only a single point of contact.

Course:
Basics of Service Design (2ECTS)
