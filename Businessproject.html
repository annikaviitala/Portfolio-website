Restaurant Business Plan

Business and Strategy | School Project

A 6 month school project, that's main goals were learning about strategy thinking, creating a business and the processes that are included. I executed this project together with three classmates with equal division of workload.

My tasks included:
Identifying the business enviroment for a new restaurant in Otaniemi.
Creating an attractive operating model for our target group and process models for business functions.
Evaluating financial potential, risks and revenue.
