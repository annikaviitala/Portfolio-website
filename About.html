About me

Hi, I'm Annika Viitala!

Information Networks student in Aalto University with a passion for great customer experiences and design.
Interested in brand, design strategy and making a real impact.
Experience in sales, communications and design.

At this moment I'm involved in a Aaltoes project called Dash Design. Our goal is to educate people about design thinking and encourage them to use it in all aspects of life. There my role is crafting program and content for Dash Talks - our conference, and other channels. Our values reflect sustainability, inclusion and creativity and that is the set of values I want to be working with in the future.

On my free time I love to spend time outdoors, knitting, reading or with friends.
During summer I usually have some type of garden plot where I grow tomatos, cucumbers etc.
